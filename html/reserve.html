Make a reservation

To make a reservation for one of our apartments, please contact the owner via email at kristijan.cinkopan@gmail.com. Ensure to include the following details in your email:

  • Provide your preferred check-in and check-out dates (refer to the calendar on the apartment page to make sure it's available)
