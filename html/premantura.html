Accommodation

You will be staying in a charming, two-story building, where the newly renovated apartment awaits you on the second floor. The apartment spans 30 m2 and comfortably accommodates two adults. It features a bathroom with a walk-in shower, a fully functional kitchen, a delightful balcony, and storage space. Inside, you'll find a flat-screen TV, air conditioning, and free Wi-Fi among other things. The apartment is positioned in a peaceful, peripheral neighborhood of a small tourist town, surrounded by exceptional natural beauty.

Additionally, we offer the option to upgrade your experience by availing our garage, bikes, paddleboard or kayak for an extra fee.

Rt Kamenjak

Experience the wonders of Nature Park Rt Kamenjak, located a mere 300 m away from the apartment. Embrace the closeness to nature's beauty and the pristine waters of the Adriatic Sea. Discover the freedom of exploring the park on bicycles or by foot, with a free entry to the nature park's breathtaking landscapes. Embark on a journey to uncover some of the most beautiful wild beaches, as you venture along the coast and encounter over 30 serene bays.
Inside the Nature Park, you can find a unique place called Safari Bar, where you can enjoy refreshing drinks and delicious grill while being captivated by a mesmerizing view. The bar's setting is an experience within itself, harmoniously blending with the breathtaking surroundings. We highly recommend visiting at least once if you find yourself in the area.

Autocamp Stupice

Between the protected landscape of Upper and Lower Kamenjak, next to Premantura village and only twelve kilometres for the urban centre of Pula, Arena Stupice Campsite found its place. In a safe embrace of an old pine forest and the endless blue of the Adriatic Sea, the campsite stands out with its small coves and rocky and pebbly beaches that gradually descend towards the sea. Guests can choose to camp in bright sunshine or mild shade, on pitches or in mobile homes. Within the camping ground, there is a windsurfing school, as well as the possibility to rent various sports equipment.
